Why Families Choose SSC

Five reasons parents and students trust Swiss School Consulting.

01

Dedicated Personal Support

Every student has a named guardian who knows them personally. From homemade birthday cake delivery to multilingual parent liaison officers (Chinese, Russian, German, French, Spanish) — our personal touch is unmatched.

02

Rapid Emergency Response

Clear procedures and local coverage ensure prompt assistance whenever issues arise — whether related to health, travel, accommodation, or school matters.

03

Safeguarding-First Approach

Guardians are trained in safeguarding, maintain regular contact with students, and work with Safehire.ai — whose AI-powered background checks identify risks beyond traditional DBS screening.

04

Transparent Communication

Parents receive regular updates and have direct access to key contacts. Clear reporting, timely notifications, and responsive support keep families informed and involved.

05

Quality Over Quantity

By limiting the number of students per guardian, we provide a more personalised experience, stronger oversight, and a higher level of care than high-volume providers.
